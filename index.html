Unworthy Thor

4 of 6

In Infinity War, Thor throws his hammer-ax at Thanos’s chest just as the dust-happy villain obtains the last Infinity Stone. “You should’ve aimed for the head,” he tells Thor, before snapping his fingers and ending half of humanity as we know it.

In Endgame, a deeply regretful Thor makes sure to decapitate the Mad Titan, though it doesn’t make him feel much better.

A MCU take on a “unworthy” Thor, totally unmoored and having lost 98% of the people he loves.

Which is a nod to the "Unworthy Thor" run by Jason Aaron.

When Thanos from the past travels to the future, these three iconic Avengers walked out together to take him on. It's the heroes who started it all back teaming up once again in this climactic fight.

They play off each other extremely well but Thanos holds his own, making this a nail-biting and extremely intense fight.

They've come a long way from fighting each other in the original Avengers movie.

In the movie, when the others are defeated and Cap being the only one capable to take on Thanos' entire army, when suddenly portals begin opening bringing every conceivable hero from the MCU to Cap's side.

It’s a tricky fight, but Thanos does prevail. Before Thanos could snap every single living being, once again 2 people seized their chances.

In the comics, Nebula seizes her chance to grab the Infinity Gauntlet. When suddenly the heroes stopped her before she got overwhelmed by this power.
